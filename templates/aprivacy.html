Privacy Policy

QuizApp, is committed to protect your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled securely and responsibly. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and share information when you visit QuizApp.

Information QuizApp Collects
QuizApp only collects your login and password. Details regarding your quiz score are not stored anywhere.

Where QuizApp uses this information
This information is used only to let you in to the current session so QuizApp knows how many people registered and how many times.

No information is shared with anyone. You as a user have the right to: Access, update, or delete your personal information. Opt out of email marketing and certain data collection practices. Request details about how your data is stored and used.
